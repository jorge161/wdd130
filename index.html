Honduras

Honduras is a country located in Central America, known for its rich culture and natural beauty. Its capital is Tegucigalpa, and its economy is based on agriculture, manufacturing, and tourism. The country has coasts on both the Caribbean Sea and the Pacific Ocean, giving it great geographical diversity. It is famous for the Mayan ruins of Copán, the beaches of Roatán, and its biodiversity. Although it faces economic and social challenges, its people are known for their warmth and traditions.
